Biography

/ / Ronaldo Suarez / /

I was first introduced to tech when I started playing small games on my dad’s Nintendo 64, and later his old computer in Cuba; he had two really old computers that ran on Windows98 which is an operating system. An operating system is what separates the user from the complexity of the code and hardware of the computer. My dad happened to have an emulator on the computer that had a bunch of games in it that I would play every single day after school. Sometimes I would even invite my neighborhood friends over to play with them. At the time I didn’t realize just how much this computer would influence my entire life.


When I was in Elementary School my only interests were drawing and painting which I had practiced as a hobby for several years. As I got into computers more and more I found out that all the drawing I would have ever wanted to, I could also do it digitally when I found out about Photoshop. I started learning and practicing on my own by giving myself constant new challenges for about two years in Photoshop. During those two years I was also taking Art and Painting classes and about three months into the classes I decided that I enjoyed the digital side a whole lot more so I got removed from the classes and focused on messing around in the software honing and refining my skills.


Once I reached my last year of middle school, I went into a digital media design class where I met Mr. Pate, he helped me and the rest of his students understand Photoshop as a whole, I decided to stay for high school in the same school. In 9th and 10th grade we continued to refine our skills in Photoshop however, we also got introduced to another program called Adobe Illustrator which instead of using pixels it used vectors and lines. Mid 10th grade I got my first certification, which was in Adobe Illustrator. I hope to become a graphic and web designer, and get a job in those fields which is what I enjoy to do, recently I started teaching myself HTML and CSS coding, and I want to continue learning more about it. I hope to someday become a web developer and professional graphic designer.
